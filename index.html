Abouts Us

Happy Hands Manufacture and export .,JSC locates in the Northern part of Vietnam, in close proximity to the capital - Hanoi - a city of rich cultural, historical and traditional values. Happy Hands is home to skilled local artisans who master the traditional technique that passes through generations after generations.Every product is delicately crafted with perfection moving through every weave. From crude materials to the finished products, every step of the making process is done by the hands of the people. With every touch instilled with diligence, love, and care, we hope to reach the heart of our valued customers. Despite the many merits that technology brings us, we hold a strong belief that only handmade products are capable of conveying happiness, from the artisans to the end users. This, is the core value based on which we established Happy Hands. We work mainly with natural materials such as bamboo, rattan, seagrass, water hyacinth, palm leaf, etc.

At Happy Hands, we offer a wide range of product catagories, from Straw bag, Straw hat, Pet House, Banneton Proof Basket, Banneton Proofing Basket (Bread Basket), Bamboo Straw, to Home Decor and beyond. Not only eye-pleasing and long-lasting durability, every product is an achievement of sustainable development in terms of economy, environment, and society. From Local to Global: we cater to the all specifics coming from customers around the globe: North America, South America, Europe, Asia, Africa, etc.At Happy Hands, we strictly comply with trade standards, guaranteeing our customers receive first-class quality products, punctual and frustration-free delivery process, exclusive deals and more!

Why Choose Us

TRADITIONAL VILLAGE

Happy Hands Company is located in Chuong My Craft village – where there are more than 10,000 craftswomen with more than 300 years of tradition. Happy Hands is home to one team of highly skilled artisans that strives to achieve great craftsmanship. We can make millions of handmade products every year.

QUALITY CONTROL

We strictly control quality at each product stage. From choosing materials, knitting style, product size, color, moisture, label, packaging … All aspects of one finished product are examined before loading on the shipping container.

DESIGN

We employ both traditional and modern techniques to create everyday items that speak to your aesthetic sense by using sustainable materials and near-to-perfection artistry. All specifics and requirements will be fulfilled timely and in ways that best benefit our many valued customers.

MANY YEARS OF EXPERIENCE DIRECT EXPORT

We are a member of ITC organization. We have many years of experiences exporting directly to many countries around the world such as US, Europe, Australia, Canada, Japan, Korea, the Middle East … Professional and fast documentation.
